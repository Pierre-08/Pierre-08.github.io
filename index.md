## Research experience

### Master’s internship — CRBE, Toulouse, France

My Master’s internship focused on the role of intraspecific trait variability in the functional diversity of freshwater fish communities from French Guiana.

This project aimed to assess how individual-level morphological variability influences estimates of functional space and functional richness in tropical freshwater fish assemblages.

Main tasks included:

- Morphometric data analysis
- Functional trait analysis
- Quantification of intraspecific trait variability
- Comparison between individual-level and species-centroid approaches
- Functional diversity analyses
- Statistical analyses in R
- Scientific writing and oral presentation

### Internship — Stockholm University, Sweden

During my internship at Stockholm University, I worked on predator–prey interactions and the ecological role of canopy-forming vegetation in coastal ecosystems.

This project investigated how vegetation cover and predator communities influence the survival of gammarids.

Main tasks included:

- Fieldwork in coastal ecosystems
- Experimental tethering of gammarids
- Analysis of predator effects
- Assessment of vegetation cover as a refuge habitat
- Statistical modelling in R

### Marine biology internship — Wimereux Marine Station, France

I completed a marine biology internship at the Wimereux Marine Station, where I worked on benthic macrofauna, macroflora, and foraminifera identification.

### Coral reef monitoring project — Bali, Indonesia

I participated in a project focused on the ecological impacts of discarded fishing gear on coral reefs and methods for monitoring these impacts.

Main activities included coral reef monitoring, hard coral identification, nudibranch identification, SCUBA-based fieldwork, and database curation.
